Our Story

Born Out of a Simple Frustration

It started the same way it probably started for you — opening the trash can lid and being hit with a smell that no amount of bags should ever produce. After one too many times cleaning out grime, maggots, and mystery residue with a hose and a prayer, we decided there had to be a better way.

So we built one. Can Cleaning Co. was founded right here in Dallas by a family who got tired of the same problem every homeowner faces: trash cans are used every single week, but almost never actually cleaned.

We invested in professional-grade equipment, developed a simple curbside service model, and started helping our neighbors. What began as a local solution quickly became something Dallas families actually look forward to — because once you experience clean cans, you never want to go back.
